Company History

It all began on August 1, 2001, with a vision rooted in love. Founded by Boss Oscar as a gift for his wife Elizabeth Ty, Zhanlin Marketing has grown from a team of 9 into a company of over 150 employees serving more than 5,000 partners. We continue to bring essential home and personal care products across Samar and Tacloban, helping communities thrive.
